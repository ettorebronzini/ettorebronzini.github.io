Bio

I am a high-energy astrophysicist currently working as a fixed-term researcher at the INAF–Osservatorio di Astrofisica e Scienza dello Spazio (OAS) in Bologna, Italy.

My research focuses on the physics of relativistic jets launched by supermassive black holes, studied through a combination of observational data and theoretical modeling. I am particularly interested in particle acceleration processes, jet–disc coupling, and multiwavelength variability in active galactic nuclei.

I obtained my PhD in Astrophysics working on high-energy emission processes in AGN jets, and I have been involved in several national and international collaborations.

Research interests

  • Relativistic jets and AGN
  • High-energy emission mechanisms
  • Particle acceleration
  • Multiwavelength variability
